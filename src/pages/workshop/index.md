- [John Armour](https://www.law.ox.ac.uk/people/john-armour) - Oxford Law
- [Sylvie Delacroix](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/staff/profiles/law/delacroix-sylvie.aspx) - Birmingham Law & Alan Turing Institute
